About the Location


Matum Hotel & Casino is located in the beautiful city of Santiago de los Caballeros. Just 30 minutes from the Cibao International Airport.

In the hotel surroundings you will find places of interest such as the Santiago Monument, The Restoration Heroes Museum, The Fort of San Luis Historic Museum, and Gran Teatro del Cibao. Also nearby are the Santiago Cathedral and the Hermanos Patiño Bridge.
